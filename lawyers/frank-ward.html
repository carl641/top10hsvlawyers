About Frank Ward

Frank Ward is a formidable criminal defense and DUI attorney who has been protecting the rights of the accused in Huntsville, Alabama, for more than 18 years. As the founder of Frank Ward Law, he has established himself as one of the most experienced and effective defense lawyers operating in Madison County and throughout North Alabama. His practice is built on a straightforward philosophy: every person charged with a crime deserves aggressive, knowledgeable legal representation, regardless of the severity of the allegations against them.

Criminal charges can upend a person's life in an instant. A DUI arrest on Memorial Parkway, a drug possession charge following a traffic stop in Madison County, or a felony indictment in the Madison County Circuit Court can carry consequences that extend far beyond fines and jail time. Loss of driving privileges, damage to professional reputation, employment difficulties, and strained family relationships are just some of the collateral effects that criminal defendants in Huntsville face. Frank Ward understands the gravity of these situations and works tirelessly to minimize the impact on his clients' lives.

Frank's depth of experience in the Huntsville criminal court system gives him a significant strategic advantage. He has tried cases before virtually every judge in Madison County, and he has built professional relationships with prosecutors, law enforcement officers, and court personnel throughout the jurisdiction. This familiarity with the local legal landscape allows him to anticipate the prosecution's strategies, identify weaknesses in the state's evidence, and negotiate effectively on behalf of his clients. When a case demands it, Frank is equally prepared to take it to trial and fight for an acquittal before a Madison County jury.

Practice Areas

Frank Ward focuses his practice exclusively on criminal defense and DUI defense, providing concentrated expertise in the following areas throughout Huntsville and North Alabama:

  • DUI Defense: First-offense DUI, repeat DUI, aggravated DUI, underage DUI, commercial vehicle DUI, and refusal cases. Frank challenges field sobriety tests, breathalyzer results, blood test procedures, and the legality of traffic stops conducted by Huntsville police and Madison County law enforcement.
  • Drug Crimes: Possession of controlled substances, possession with intent to distribute, drug trafficking, marijuana offenses, and prescription drug charges in Madison County and throughout the Northern District of Alabama.
  • Felony Defense: Assault, robbery, burglary, theft, weapons charges, white-collar crimes, and other felony offenses prosecuted in Madison County Circuit Court.
  • Misdemeanor Defense: Assault, theft, trespassing, disorderly conduct, domestic violence, and other misdemeanor charges heard in Huntsville Municipal Court and Madison County District Court.
  • Traffic Violations: Reckless driving, driving on a suspended license, hit and run, and other serious traffic offenses in Huntsville and Madison County.
  • Expungement: Petition-based record sealing and expungement proceedings under Alabama law to help clients clear their criminal records and move forward with their lives.

Case Results

Frank Ward has achieved notable results in DUI and criminal defense cases throughout Huntsville and Madison County. While every case is different and past results do not guarantee future outcomes, these examples demonstrate his courtroom effectiveness:
