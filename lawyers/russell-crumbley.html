About Russell Crumbley

Russell Crumbley is a seasoned multi-practice attorney who has been a pillar of the Huntsville, Alabama, legal community for more than 20 years. As a founding partner of Crumbley-Blackwell-Price Attorneys, Russell has built one of North Alabama's most versatile law firms, offering skilled representation across divorce and family law, bankruptcy and debt relief, personal injury, and civil litigation. His ability to handle a broad range of legal matters has made him one of the most sought-after attorneys in the Huntsville metropolitan area.

What distinguishes Russell Crumbley from many attorneys in Madison County is the breadth of his legal knowledge combined with a genuinely client-centered approach to practice. Over two decades, he has represented thousands of individuals and families in Huntsville, Decatur, Athens, and throughout the Tennessee Valley. Whether a client is struggling with overwhelming debt, navigating the emotional complexities of a divorce, or recovering from injuries sustained in an automobile accident, Russell provides the same level of dedicated, personalized attention to every matter that comes through his door.

Russell's firm, Crumbley-Blackwell-Price Attorneys, has grown into a respected institution in Huntsville. The firm's collaborative approach allows clients to benefit from the collective experience of multiple attorneys while still receiving individualized service. Russell's leadership has been instrumental in establishing the firm's reputation for integrity, thoroughness, and aggressive advocacy in Madison County courts and throughout the Northern District of Alabama. Clients frequently describe him as approachable, honest, and relentless in his pursuit of favorable outcomes.

Practice Areas

Russell Crumbley's multi-disciplinary practice spans several critical areas of law, allowing him to serve the diverse needs of Huntsville residents and families across North Alabama:

  • Divorce & Family Law: Contested and uncontested divorces, property division, alimony, child custody and visitation, child support, and post-divorce modifications. Russell has handled hundreds of domestic relations cases in Madison County Family Court.
  • Bankruptcy & Debt Relief: Chapter 7 and Chapter 13 bankruptcy filings, debt negotiation, creditor harassment defense, and foreclosure prevention. He guides Huntsville families through the federal bankruptcy process with sensitivity and strategic precision.
  • Personal Injury: Car accidents, truck accidents, slip and fall injuries, premises liability, and wrongful death claims. Russell works on a contingency fee basis for injury cases, meaning clients pay nothing unless they recover compensation.
  • Civil Litigation: Contract disputes, business litigation, property disputes, and general civil matters filed in Madison County Circuit Court and other North Alabama jurisdictions.
  • Workers' Compensation: Representation of injured workers seeking benefits for workplace injuries sustained in Huntsville and throughout Alabama.
