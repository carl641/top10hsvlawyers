About Ralph W. Hornsby Jr.

Ralph W. Hornsby Jr. stands as one of the most experienced and accomplished personal injury attorneys in North Alabama. With more than 30 years of legal practice, Ralph has dedicated his career to representing individuals and families in Huntsville, Madison County, and communities throughout the Tennessee Valley who have been harmed by the negligence of others. As a partner at Hornsby, Watson & Hornsby, he leads a firm that has become synonymous with aggressive, effective advocacy for injury victims across Alabama.

Throughout his three decades of practice in Huntsville, Ralph has developed a particular expertise in nursing home abuse and neglect cases. He recognized early in his career that some of the most vulnerable members of the North Alabama community—elderly residents in long-term care facilities—were suffering in silence. Since then, he has become a leading voice in holding nursing homes, assisted living facilities, and their corporate owners accountable when they fail to provide adequate care to residents in Madison County and beyond.

Ralph's approach to the practice of law is deeply personal. He believes that every client who walks into his Huntsville office deserves to be heard, respected, and represented with the full force of his experience and resources. Whether handling a complex trucking accident case on Interstate 565 or investigating patterns of neglect at a North Alabama nursing facility, Ralph brings the same level of dedication, thoroughness, and tenacity that has defined his career for over three decades.

Practice Areas

Ralph W. Hornsby Jr. represents clients throughout the Huntsville metropolitan area and North Alabama in a range of personal injury and civil litigation matters. His primary areas of practice include:

  • Nursing Home Abuse & Neglect — Investigating and litigating cases of physical abuse, emotional abuse, neglect, bedsores, malnutrition, medication errors, and wrongful death in North Alabama nursing homes and assisted living facilities
  • Personal Injury — Representing victims of car accidents, motorcycle crashes, and pedestrian injuries throughout Huntsville and Madison County
  • Wrongful Death — Seeking justice and full compensation for families in North Alabama who have lost loved ones due to the negligence or recklessness of others
  • Trucking Accidents — Handling complex commercial vehicle and 18-wheeler accident cases on I-565, US-72, and other major roadways in the Huntsville area
  • Premises Liability — Pursuing claims for slip and fall injuries, unsafe property conditions, and negligent security at Huntsville businesses and properties
  • Medical Malpractice — Working alongside medical experts to hold healthcare providers in Madison County accountable for substandard care

Ralph's extensive trial experience gives his clients a significant advantage. Insurance companies and defense attorneys in the Huntsville area know that when Ralph Hornsby takes a case, he is fully prepared to go to trial if a fair settlement cannot be reached. This reputation has been built over 30 years of consistent results in Alabama courtrooms.
