About Bill Hall

Bill Hall is a seasoned family law attorney who has served the Huntsville, Alabama community for more than 20 years. As the founding partner of Bill Hall & Associates, P.C., Bill has guided thousands of Madison County families through some of the most challenging and emotional legal matters they will ever face. His practice encompasses the full spectrum of domestic relations law, from contested divorces and complex custody disputes to collaborative adoptions and post-divorce modifications.

Family law matters are deeply personal, and Bill understands that the decisions made during a divorce or custody proceeding will shape his clients' lives and the lives of their children for years to come. That understanding drives his approach to every case he handles in Huntsville. He takes the time to listen carefully to each client's concerns, explain the legal process in clear and accessible terms, and develop a strategy that protects their interests while keeping the focus on the best outcome for any children involved.

Over his two decades of practice in Madison County, Bill has developed an in-depth knowledge of the local family court system. He is familiar with the judges, procedures, and expectations of the Madison County Circuit Court—Domestic Relations Division. This local expertise gives his clients a meaningful advantage when navigating the complexities of Alabama family law. Whether a case involves the division of a military retirement from a career at Redstone Arsenal or a custody dispute between parents living in different parts of North Alabama, Bill brings the knowledge and experience needed to achieve the best possible result.

Bill's firm, Bill Hall & Associates, P.C., has grown over the years to become one of the most recognized family law practices in the Huntsville area. The firm's reputation is built on a foundation of integrity, compassion, and results. Clients come to Bill Hall because they know they will receive honest counsel, dedicated representation, and a commitment to resolving their family law matter as efficiently and favorably as possible.

Practice Areas

Bill Hall provides comprehensive family law representation to individuals and families throughout Huntsville, Madison County, and the greater North Alabama region. His areas of practice include:

  • Divorce — Representing clients in both contested and uncontested divorces in Madison County, including cases involving high-value assets, business interests, military benefits, and complex property division
  • Child Custody & Visitation — Advocating for the best interests of children in Huntsville custody disputes, including initial custody determinations, custody modifications, and relocation cases
  • Child Support — Establishing, modifying, and enforcing child support orders under Alabama's child support guidelines for Madison County families
  • Spousal Support & Alimony — Negotiating and litigating alimony awards in Huntsville divorce proceedings, including rehabilitative, periodic, and lump-sum alimony
  • Adoption — Guiding families in North Alabama through stepparent adoptions, agency adoptions, and private adoptions, ensuring compliance with Alabama adoption laws
  • Domestic Violence & Protective Orders — Helping victims of domestic violence in Madison County obtain Protection From Abuse (PFA) orders and navigate safety planning
  • Post-Divorce Modifications — Filing and defending petitions to modify custody, visitation, child support, and alimony orders when circumstances change for Huntsville-area families

Huntsville's significant military presence adds a unique dimension to many of the family law cases Bill handles. Military divorces involve special considerations around the Servicemembers Civil Relief Act (SCRA), division of military retirement pay under the Uniformed Services Former Spouses' Protection Act (USFSPA), and the logistics of custody arrangements when a parent is subject to deployment or relocation. Bill's extensive experience with military family law cases in the Huntsville area makes him particularly well-suited to represent families with ties to Redstone Arsenal, the Missile Defense Agency, and other military installations in North Alabama.
