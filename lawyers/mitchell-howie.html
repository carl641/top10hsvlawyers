About Mitchell J. Howie

Mitchell J. Howie is one of the most respected family law attorneys serving Huntsville, Alabama, and the broader Madison County community. With more than 15 years of dedicated experience in domestic relations and family court proceedings, Mitchell has built a reputation for providing compassionate yet assertive legal representation to individuals and families navigating some of the most challenging moments of their lives. From his office in Huntsville, he has helped hundreds of clients resolve complex family disputes with professionalism and care.

Attorney Howie's practice is rooted in a deep understanding of Alabama family law statutes and the specific procedures followed in Madison County Family Court. Whether a client is facing a contested divorce, a child custody modification, or a difficult adoption proceeding, Mitchell brings the same level of thorough preparation and strategic advocacy to every case. His clients regularly commend him for his ability to explain complex legal concepts in straightforward terms and for his genuine commitment to protecting their interests and the well-being of their children.

Huntsville has grown significantly over the past two decades, and with that growth has come an increasing demand for skilled family law practitioners who understand the unique dynamics of North Alabama families. Mitchell J. Howie has been at the forefront of this legal community, earning recognition from peers and clients alike for his courtroom effectiveness and his willingness to pursue negotiated settlements when they serve the client's best interests. He is a frequent presence in the Madison County Courthouse and maintains strong professional relationships throughout the local legal community.

Practice Areas

Mitchell J. Howie concentrates his practice in the following areas of family law, providing comprehensive legal services to families throughout the Huntsville area and North Alabama:

  • Divorce: Contested and uncontested divorces, including complex property division, alimony disputes, and high-asset marital dissolutions filed in Madison County and surrounding jurisdictions.
  • Child Custody: Initial custody determinations, custody modifications, relocation cases, and grandparent visitation rights under Alabama law.
  • Child Support: Establishment, modification, and enforcement of child support orders in accordance with Alabama's child support guidelines.
  • Adoption: Stepparent adoptions, private adoptions, and agency adoptions in the Huntsville, Alabama, area, including all required court proceedings and home study coordination.
  • Protective Orders: Representation for individuals seeking or responding to protection from abuse orders in Madison County courts.
  • Paternity Actions: Legal proceedings to establish or challenge paternity, including related custody and support matters.
