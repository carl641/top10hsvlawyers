About Joe A. King

Joe A. King is one of the most experienced and accomplished personal injury attorneys in Huntsville, Alabama. For more than 25 years, he has dedicated his career to representing individuals and families who have been injured or lost loved ones due to the negligence of others. As the founding attorney of King Law Firm, Joe has recovered millions of dollars in compensation for accident victims throughout Madison County, the Tennessee Valley, and the entire North Alabama region. His unwavering commitment to his clients and his willingness to take cases to trial when necessary have established him as one of the premier injury lawyers in the state.

Huntsville's rapid growth over the past quarter century has brought increased traffic, larger commercial vehicles on area highways, and a corresponding rise in serious motor vehicle accidents. Interstate 565, Highway 72, Memorial Parkway, and Research Park Boulevard are among the most heavily traveled roadways in Madison County, and they are also where many of the most severe collisions occur. Joe A. King has represented victims injured on these very roads, and he understands the specific challenges that Huntsville accident cases present, from dealing with multiple insurance companies to coordinating with local accident reconstruction experts and medical professionals at Huntsville Hospital and Crestwood Medical Center.

What sets Joe apart from many personal injury attorneys in North Alabama is his combination of trial experience and negotiation skill. While many injury cases settle before reaching a courtroom, insurance companies know that Joe King is fully prepared to take a case to a Madison County jury if a fair settlement cannot be reached. This reputation as a trial-ready attorney gives his clients significant leverage during settlement negotiations, often resulting in higher compensation than they would receive with an attorney who lacks courtroom experience. Joe takes all personal injury cases on a contingency fee basis, meaning his clients never pay attorney fees unless he recovers compensation for them.

Practice Areas

Joe A. King and King Law Firm focus exclusively on personal injury and wrongful death litigation, allowing them to provide specialized representation in the following areas:

  • Car Accidents: Representation for drivers, passengers, and pedestrians injured in automobile collisions throughout Huntsville, Madison County, and North Alabama, including intersection accidents, rear-end collisions, head-on crashes, and hit-and-run incidents.
  • Truck Accidents: Complex commercial vehicle accident cases involving tractor-trailers, delivery trucks, and other large vehicles on I-565, Highway 72, and other major Huntsville-area thoroughfares, including claims against trucking companies and their insurers.
  • Wrongful Death: Compassionate representation for families who have lost loved ones due to another party's negligence, including fatal car accidents, workplace fatalities, and deaths caused by defective products or medical malpractice.
  • Premises Liability: Slip and fall injuries, trip and fall accidents, inadequate security claims, and other injuries sustained on commercial properties, residential properties, and public spaces in Huntsville.
  • Product Liability: Injuries caused by defective or dangerous products, including defective automotive parts, machinery, consumer products, and pharmaceutical drugs.
  • Motorcycle & Bicycle Accidents: Representation for motorcyclists and cyclists injured by negligent drivers on Huntsville and Madison County roadways.

Case Results
