About LeTonya F. Moore

LeTonya F. Moore is a distinguished business law and education law attorney serving the Huntsville, Alabama community through her firm, Moore Law. With more than 14 years of legal experience, LeTonya has carved out a unique niche in the North Alabama legal landscape by combining her expertise in business law with a deep commitment to education law. Her dual focus allows her to serve a diverse client base that includes Huntsville entrepreneurs, small business owners, educators, school administrators, and educational institutions throughout Madison County.

Huntsville's rapid growth as one of the fastest-growing cities in Alabama has created tremendous opportunities for entrepreneurs and business owners. From technology startups in Cummings Research Park to restaurants and retail businesses along the Parkway, the Huntsville business community is thriving. LeTonya has positioned Moore Law to be the trusted legal partner for these businesses, guiding them from initial formation and entity selection through contract negotiations, regulatory compliance, and ongoing corporate governance matters.

On the education law side of her practice, LeTonya brings a passionate commitment to ensuring that students, parents, educators, and institutions in the Huntsville City Schools system and surrounding Madison County districts receive fair treatment under the law. She has extensive experience navigating the complex intersection of federal and state education regulations, administrative proceedings, and civil rights protections that shape the educational experience for families throughout North Alabama.

What sets LeTonya apart from other attorneys in the Huntsville area is her ability to see the big picture while attending to the critical details. Whether she is drafting an operating agreement for a new LLC in Madison County or advocating for a student's rights in a disciplinary hearing, she approaches every matter with the same combination of strategic thinking, thorough preparation, and genuine concern for her clients' long-term success.

Practice Areas

LeTonya F. Moore provides comprehensive legal services to businesses, entrepreneurs, educators, and families throughout the Huntsville metropolitan area. Her primary areas of practice include:

  • Business Formation & Entity Selection — Advising Huntsville entrepreneurs on the optimal business structure (LLC, corporation, partnership) and handling all formation filings with the Alabama Secretary of State
  • Contract Drafting & Negotiation — Preparing, reviewing, and negotiating business contracts, vendor agreements, partnership agreements, and commercial leases for North Alabama businesses
  • Corporate Governance & Compliance — Guiding Huntsville-area businesses through ongoing compliance requirements, board governance, operating agreement amendments, and regulatory matters
  • Education Law & Student Rights — Representing students, parents, and educators in Madison County in matters including disciplinary hearings, special education disputes (IEP/504 plans), and student civil rights issues
  • School Administrative Proceedings — Advocating for clients before school boards, administrative agencies, and in state-level education proceedings throughout North Alabama
  • Employment Law for Small Businesses — Helping Huntsville small business owners navigate employee handbooks, employment agreements, termination procedures, and workplace compliance with Alabama and federal employment laws

LeTonya's practice reflects the unique needs of the Huntsville community. As the city continues to grow and attract new businesses and families, the demand for skilled legal counsel in both business and education law has grown as well. Moore Law is positioned to meet that demand with responsive, client-focused service.
